Delete Your Account & Data

一起早睡 · Sleep Early Together

Effective date: May 30, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated data for the app “一起早睡 Sleep Early Together”.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (instant)

  1. Open 一起早睡 Sleep Early Together and sign in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Enter your password to confirm.

Your account and associated data are deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app, email yujiwork@outlook.com from the email address associated with your account, with the subject “Delete my account.” We will verify and delete your account within 30 days.

What is deleted and what is kept

DataWhat happens
Account / email addressPermanently deleted
Sleep check-in recordsPermanently deleted
Friend connections & requestsPermanently deleted

We do not retain your personal data after deletion. Any residual copies in routine encrypted system backups are purged within 30 days. We keep no data beyond that period.

Questions? Email yujiwork@outlook.com.

删除你的账户与数据

本页面说明如何为应用 “一起早睡 Sleep Early Together” 申请删除你的账户及相关数据。

方式一 —— 在应用内删除（即时）

  1. 打开 一起早睡 Sleep Early Together 并登录。
  2. 进入 “我” 标签页。
  3. 点击 删除账户。
  4. 输入密码以确认。

你的账户及相关数据将被立即删除。

方式二 —— 通过邮件申请删除

如果你已无法访问应用，请使用账户对应的邮箱发送邮件至 yujiwork@outlook.com， 主题写明 “Delete my account”（删除我的账户）。我们将在 30 天内 验证并删除你的账户。

哪些数据会被删除 / 保留

数据处理方式
账户 / 邮箱地址永久删除
睡眠打卡记录永久删除
好友关系与好友请求永久删除
