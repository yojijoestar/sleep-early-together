Privacy Policy

一起早睡 · Sleep Early Together

Effective date: May 30, 2026

Sleep Early Together (“the App”, “we”, “us”) helps you build a healthy sleep routine by checking in at bedtime and staying accountable with friends. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Account information: your email address, which you provide when you create an account.
  • Activity you create in the App: your sleep check-ins (the date and time you check in) and the friend connections you make.

We do not collect your precise location, contacts, photos, or advertising identifiers, and we do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

How we use your information

  • To create and secure your account.
  • To record your check-ins and show your progress.
  • To let you connect with friends and see shared check-in activity.

How your data is stored

Your data is stored using Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore), operated by Google. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections and protected by access rules. See Google’s privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Your email address is not shared with other users. When you add friends, your display name and check-in activity may be visible to the friends you connect with.

Data retention and deletion

You may delete your account at any time from within the App, or by emailing us at the address below. When your account is deleted, your email address and associated check-in data are removed from our systems.

Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at yujiwork@outlook.com.

隐私政策

“一起早睡 Sleep Early Together”（以下称“本应用”“我们”）帮助你通过睡前打卡、与好友互相监督，养成健康的睡眠习惯。本政策说明我们收集哪些信息，以及如何使用这些信息。

我们收集的信息

  • 账户信息：你在注册时提供的电子邮箱地址。
  • 你在应用中产生的数据：你的睡眠打卡记录（打卡的日期与时间）以及你添加的好友关系。

我们不会收集你的精确位置、通讯录、照片或广告标识符，也不使用任何第三方广告或分析 SDK。

我们如何使用这些信息

  • 创建并保护你的账户。
  • 记录你的打卡并展示你的进度。
  • 让你与好友互相关注并查看共享的打卡动态。

数据的存储方式

你的数据通过 Google 提供的 Firebase（Firebase 身份验证与 Cloud Firestore）存储。数据通过加密连接传输，并受访问规则保护。详见 Google 隐私政策： policies.google.com/privacy。

信息共享

我们不会出售或出租你的个人信息。你的邮箱不会向其他用户公开。当你添加好友后，你的显示名称与打卡动态可能会对你添加的好友可见。

数据保留与删除

你可以随时在应用内删除账户，或发送邮件联系我们。账户删除后，你的邮箱地址及相关打卡数据将从我们的系统中移除。

儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童，我们不会在知情的情况下收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。

政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本政策。重大变更将通过更新上方的生效日期予以体现。

联系我们

如对本政策有任何疑问，请发送邮件至 yujiwork@outlook.com。
